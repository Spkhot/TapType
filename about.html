From Frustration to Flow

It started with a simple observation: why do we still rely on the archaic Shift key? In an age of smart devices and intuitive interfaces, constantly reaching for a modifier key felt clumsy and inefficient.

TapType is the answer. It's a lightweight Windows utility that eliminates the need for Shift. Tap for lowercase, hold for UPPERCASE. It’s seamless, fast, and efficient.

Our goal is to create software that feels like a natural extension of your thoughts—powerful, invisible, and incredibly efficient.

Our Core Philosophy

Radical Simplicity
