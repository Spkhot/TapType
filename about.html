Founders

TapType.

From Frustration to Flow

It started with a simple observation: why do we still rely on the archaic Shift key? In an age of smart devices and intuitive interfaces, constantly reaching for a modifier key felt clumsy and inefficient.

TapType is the answer. It's a lightweight Windows utility that intelligently distinguishes between a tap for lowercase and a hold for uppercase, eliminating the need for the Shift key.

Our goal is to create software that feels like a natural extension of your thoughts—powerful, invisible, and efficient.

Our Core Philosophy

Everything we build is guided by these three principles.

Radical Simplicity

No complex settings or UI. Install it, and it just works. Control is simple and accessible from the system tray when you need it.

Peak Performance

Built in C++ for minimal resource usage (<5MB RAM). TapType never slows your system down, ensuring a snappy experience.

User-Centric Design

We prioritize your comfort and workflow. Reducing awkward hand movements makes typing more pleasant and sustainable long-term.
